* Question
Extend the evaluator in this section to support the special form `let`. (See exercise 4.6.)

* Answer
** Implementation
:PROPERTIES:
:header-args:scheme: :eval no
:END:
#+begin_src scheme :tangle 4.22.analyzingmceval.eval.let.scm
  (define (install-let-package)
    (put 'eval 'let
         (lambda (exp)
           (analyze (let->combination exp))))
    'ok)
#+end_src
