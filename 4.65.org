* Question
:PROPERTIES:
:header-args:scheme: :eval no
:END:
Cy D. Fect, looking forward to the day when he will rise in the organization, gives a query to find all the wheels (using the ~wheel~ rule of section 4.4.1):

#+begin_src scheme
  (wheel ?who)
#+end_src

To his surprise, the system responds

#+begin_src scheme
  ;;; Query results:
  (wheel (Warbucks Oliver))
  (wheel (Bitdiddle Ben))
  (wheel (Warbucks Oliver))
  (wheel (Warbucks Oliver))
  (wheel (Warbucks Oliver))
#+end_src

Why is Oliver Warbucks listed four times?

* Answer
Oliver Warbucks shows up four times because he supervises Scrooge Eben (who supervises Robert Cratchet) and Ben Bitdiddle (who supervises Lem E. Tweakit, Cy D. Fect, and Alyssa P. Hacker).
