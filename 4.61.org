* Question
:PROPERTIES:
:header-args:scheme: :eval no
:END:
The following rules implement a next-to relation that finds adjacent elements of a list:

#+begin_src scheme
  (rule (?x next-to ?y in (?x ?y . ?u)))

  (rule (?x next-to ?y in (?v . ?z))
        (?x next-to ?y in ?z))
#+end_src

What will the response be to the following queries?

#+begin_src scheme
  (?x next-to ?y in (1 (2 3) 4))

  (?x next-to 1 in (2 1 3 1))
#+end_src

* Answer
#+begin_src scheme :results output :exports both
  ;; Load
  (load "4.query.scm")
  ;; Test
  (for-each mc-eval
            (list '(assert! (rule (?x next-to ?y in (?x ?y . ?u))))
                  '(assert! (rule (?x next-to ?y in (?v . ?z))
                                  (?x next-to ?y in ?z)))
                  '(?x next-to ?y in (1 (2 3) 4))
                  '(?x next-to 1 in (2 1 3 1))))
#+end_src
