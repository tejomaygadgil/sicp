* Question
:PROPERTIES:
:header-args:scheme: :eval no
:END:
Why does ~flatten-stream~ use ~delay~ explicitly? What would be wrong with defining it as follows:

#+begin_src scheme
(define (flatten-stream stream)
  (if (stream-null? stream)
      the-empty-stream
      (interleave
       (stream-car stream)
       (flatten-stream (stream-cdr stream)))))
#+end_src

* Answer
Without the delay, ~(flatten-stream (stream-cdr stream))~ would evaluate before being sent to ~interleave~, causing a potentially infinite loop.
